Backyard Plants Guide

Explore common edible plants found in Filipino backyards. Learn how to identify them and understand their nutritional value.

🌱 Malunggay

Rich in vitamins A, C, and calcium. Helps boost immunity and improve nutrition.

🌱 Kangkong

A leafy vegetable rich in iron and fiber. Good for digestion and overall health.

🌱 Alugbati

Contains vitamins and antioxidants that help maintain healthy skin and body functions.

🌱 Talbos ng Kamote (Sweet Potato Leaves)

A nutritious leafy vegetable rich in antioxidants, fiber, and vitamins. Helps improve digestion and supports heart health.

🌱 Ampalaya (Bitter Gourd)

Known for helping regulate blood sugar levels. It is rich in vitamins and is commonly used in Filipino dishes.

🌱 Sitaw (String Beans)

A good source of fiber and vitamins that support digestion and overall body function. Easy to grow in warm climates.

🌱 Pechay (Bok Choy)

Rich in calcium, iron, and vitamins A and C. Helps strengthen bones and boost immunity.

🌱 Okra

Contains fiber and antioxidants that support digestion and heart health. It grows well in tropical environments.

These plants are easy to grow, affordable, and packed with nutrients—perfect for everyday meals.
