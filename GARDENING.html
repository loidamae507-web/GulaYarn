Gardening Tips

Growing your own plants is a rewarding and sustainable practice. It not only provides fresh food but also helps reduce expenses and promotes a healthier lifestyle. Here are some simple tips to help you start your backyard garden.

🌱 Basic Tips

  • Water your plants regularly, especially during dry days
  • Ensure your plants receive enough sunlight (at least 4–6 hours daily)
  • Use healthy, nutrient-rich soil
  • Remove weeds to prevent competition for nutrients
  • Use natural fertilizers like compost when possible

🌿 Plant Examples

Kangkong

Thrives in moist soil and requires frequent watering. It grows quickly and can be harvested multiple times.

Malunggay

A low-maintenance plant that can grow even in dry conditions. It requires minimal care and can survive in poor soil.

🌼 Additional Advice

Start with a few plants and gradually expand your garden. Reuse containers like plastic bottles or pots if you have limited space. Gardening is not about perfection—it’s about consistency and care.

With patience and care, your backyard can become a source of fresh and healthy food.
