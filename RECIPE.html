Filipino Vegetable Recipes

Turn your backyard plants into delicious and healthy meals with these easy recipes.

🌱 Tinolang Manok with Malunggay

Ingredients

  • Chicken, Malunggay leaves, Green papaya or sayote, Garlic, onion, ginger, Fish sauce, Water

Steps

  1. Sauté garlic, onion, and ginger.
  2. Add chicken and cook until lightly browned.
  3. Add fish sauce and water; simmer until chicken is tender.
  4. Add papaya or sayote.
  5. Add malunggay leaves last and cook for 1 minute.

🌿 Adobong Kangkong

Ingredients

  • Kangkong, Garlic and onion, Soy sauce, Vinegar, Oyster sauce (optional)

Steps

  1. Wash and cut kangkong.
  2. Sauté garlic and onion.
  3. Add soy sauce and a little water.
  4. Add kangkong stems first, then leaves.
  5. Pour vinegar and let simmer without stirring.

🍃 Laswa with Alugbati

Ingredients

  • Alugbati, Squash, Sitaw, Okra, Eggplant, Shrimp/Fish, Salt/Bagoong

Steps

  1. Boil water with onions and tomatoes.
  2. Add the squash and cook until slightly tender.
  3. Add the sitaw, okra, and eggplant.
  4. Add the shrimp or fish and season with salt.
  5. Add the alugbati leaves last and simmer for 1-2 minutes.

🥗 Laswa with Talbos ng Kamote

Ingredients

  • Talbos ng Kamote, Saluyot, Squash, Sitaw, Shrimp/Fish, Ginger, Onion

Steps

  1. In a pot, boil water with ginger and onion.
  2. Put in the squash and sitaw; cook until soft.
  3. Add the shrimp or fish for flavor.
  4. Gently stir in the talbos ng kamote leaves.
  5. Cover and simmer briefly until leaves wilt.

🌿 Ginisang Kulitis

Ingredients

  • Kulitis leaves, Garlic, Onion, Tomatoes, Salt and pepper

Steps

  1. Sauté garlic, onion, and tomatoes.
  2. Add kulitis leaves.
  3. Cook until wilted.
  4. Season to taste and serve hot.

🥥 Laing (Gabi Leaves)

Ingredients

  • Dried gabi leaves, Coconut milk, Aromatics, Chili, Bagoong

Steps

  1. Sauté garlic, onion, and ginger.
  2. Pour coconut milk and simmer gently.
  3. Add gabi leaves without stirring too much.
  4. Add bagoong and chili; cook until creamy.

🌿 Adobong Takway (Gabi Runners)

Ingredients

  • Takway (Gabi runners), Aromatics, Soy sauce, Vinegar, Bagoong, Pork bits/Shrimp, Chili

Steps

  1. Clean takway and cut into 2-inch pieces.
  2. Sauté garlic, onion, and pork/shrimp until fragrant.
  3. Add takway and stir-fry briefly.
  4. Add soy sauce and water; simmer until tender.
  5. Add vinegar and chili; let it simmer without stirring.

🍳 Ginisang Ampalaya with Egg

Ingredients

  • Ampalaya, Egg, Garlic, onion, tomato

Steps

  1. Slice ampalaya thinly and soak in salt water.
  2. Sauté garlic, onion, and tomato.
  3. Add ampalaya and cook briefly.
  4. Add beaten eggs and stir gently.

🥓 Adobong Sitaw

Ingredients

  • Sitaw, Garlic, Pork (optional), Soy sauce, Vinegar

Steps

  1. Sauté garlic and pork. Add sitaw.
  2. Pour soy sauce and water.
  3. Add vinegar last and simmer.

🍅 Ginisang Okra

Ingredients

  • Okra, Garlic, onion, tomato, Shrimp paste or fish sauce

Steps

  1. Wash and trim okra.
  2. Sauté garlic, onion, and tomato.
  3. Add okra and cook briefly.
  4. Season with bagoong and serve.

🥥 Ginataang Tambo (Bamboo Shoots)

Ingredients

  • Tambo, Coconut milk, Saluyot/Alugbati, Corn, Shrimp/Tinapa, Ginger, Garlic, Onion

Steps

  1. Boil tambo for 15-20 mins; drain.
  2. Sauté aromatics and shrimp/fish.
  3. Pour in coconut milk and simmer.
  4. Add tambo and corn; simmer until thickened.
  5. Add leaves last and season.
