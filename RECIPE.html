Simple Backyard Recipes

Turn your backyard plants into delicious and healthy meals with these easy recipes.

🥘 Malunggay Soup

Ingredients:
  • Malunggay leaves
  • Garlic
  • Onion
  • Broth
Procedure:

Sauté garlic and onion, add broth, then mix in malunggay leaves. Simmer and serve.

🥗 Kangkong Salad

Ingredients:
  • Kangkong
  • Tomato
  • Onion
  • Vinegar
Procedure:

Boil kangkong, mix with vegetables, and add vinegar dressing.

🍳 Ginisang Ampalaya

Ingredients:
  • Ampalaya
  • Egg
  • Garlic
  • Onion
Procedure:

Sauté garlic and onion, add sliced ampalaya, then mix in beaten egg. Cook until done.

🍲 Tinolang Manok with Malunggay

Ingredients:
  • Chicken
  • Malunggay leaves
  • Ginger
  • Garlic
  • Onion
Procedure:

Boil chicken with ginger, garlic, and onion. Add malunggay leaves before serving.

🥬 Adobong Sitaw

Ingredients:
  • Sitaw
  • Soy sauce
  • Vinegar
  • Garlic
Procedure:

Cook sitaw with garlic, soy sauce, and vinegar until tender.

🍛 Ginisang Pechay

Ingredients:
  • Pechay
  • Garlic
  • Onion
  • Oyster sauce (optional)
Procedure:

Sauté garlic and onion, add pechay, then season and cook briefly.

🍲 Sinigang na Okra

Ingredients:
  • Okra
  • Tamarind mix
  • Tomato
  • Onion
Procedure:

Boil water with tomato and onion, add tamarind mix, then add okra and simmer.

Healthy meals can start right from your backyard—simple, affordable, and nutritious.
