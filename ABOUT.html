Grow Local, Eat Healthy.

GULAYarn is a platform dedicated to helping Filipinos rediscover the value of backyard gardening and local vegetables. Many nutritious plants grow easily in our surroundings, yet they are often overlooked. Through simple guides, easy recipes, and practical gardening tips, GULAYarn encourages families to grow their own food, eat healthier meals, and live more sustainably.

Whether you are a beginner or someone with experience in gardening, this website provides accessible information to help you start your journey toward healthier living—right from your own backyard.

  • 🌿 Discover edible backyard plants commonly found in the Philippines
  • 🥗 Learn their health benefits and nutritional value
  • 🍲 Cook affordable and easy home recipes
  • 🌱 Grow your own vegetables with simple gardening techniques

🌱 Fun Fact

Many common backyard plants in the Philippines—such as Malunggay, Kangkong, and Saluyot—are considered “survival vegetables” because they grow easily even in poor soil and can be harvested repeatedly, making them a reliable source of food and nutrition for Filipino families.

🌱 Featured Plant: Malunggay (Moringa)

Known as a “miracle tree,” malunggay is packed with essential nutrients such as vitamins A, C, and E, calcium, and antioxidants. It helps strengthen the immune system, improve digestion, and support overall wellness. It is also easy to grow, making it perfect for home gardens.
